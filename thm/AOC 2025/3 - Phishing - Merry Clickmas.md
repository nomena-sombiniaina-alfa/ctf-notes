## Social Engineering
Human-targeted manipulation. Key drivers: urgency, curiosity, authority.

## Phishing types
* **Email phishing**
* **Smishing** (SMS)
* **Vishing** (voice calls)
* **Quishing** (QR codes)
  Goal: trick user into clicking/opening/replying → steal creds, money, access.

## STOP methods : https://www.allthingssecured.com/tips/email-phishing-scams-stop-method/
### STOP 1
* **S**uspicious?
* **T**elling me to click?
* **O**ffering amazing deal?
* **P**ushing urgency?

### STOP 2
* **S**low down
* **T**ype URL manually
* **O**pen nothing unexpected
* **P**rove sender identity

**Delivery (SET)**
SET → Social-Engineering Attacks → Mass Mailer → Single Email.
Send realistic email (spoofed sender), subject “Shipping Schedule Changes”, include malicious link.
